Current Developments

lifTable

In the home, people have their favorite place to work, yet it is not always at the best height.

lifTable is a portable adapter that can be slide under your work surface to raise it several centimeters higher.

If you're seeking a way to take a break from sitting without reseting your space, sign up.

Send me the lifTable update.

shaRed

Life in the home is much more connected with those that we live. Sometimes, people wish to use the same place when it is going to be used.

shaRed is an automated notification board that coordinates schedules to alert people when a home space is going to be used as well as in use.

Frustrated by preventable and spontaneous interruptions? We will keep you informed about shaRed

Send me the shaRed update.
